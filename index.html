About Us

Tomorrow’s Future Today’s Youth Foundation is a Non-Profit Organization with Public Benefit Organization established in February 2023. It was founded by Mr Rofhiwa Ramadolela and Pastor Nthabiseng Sekhu. who are currently co-Executive Directors of the Organization.

The pair is passionate and devoted to the development, improvement and change in the youth of this generation. Tomorrow’s Future Today’s Youth Foundation; through skills development programs, motivational events, interactional events, youth communication events, youth appreciation events and youth entrepreneurship concepts, to empower basic skills to youth to develop into independent thinkers and skilled individuals in the societies.

Aligned with the country’s strategic goal and priorities, the organization particularly focuses on the development of youth in each and every location with disadvantaged youth such as townships and rural areas
